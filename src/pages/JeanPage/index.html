Character Biografy

Jean Gunnhildr is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact.

The daugher of Frederica Gunnhildr and Seamus Pegg, the older sister of Barbara, and a descendant of the prestigious Gunnhildr Clan, Jean is Acting Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius. She is always busy handling unrest across Mondstadt and tirelessly working to maintain the City of Freedon.

Gale Blade Elemental Skill

Focusing the might of the formless wind around her blade, Jean releases a miniature storm, launching opponents in the direction she aims at, dealing massive Anemo DMG.


Hold

At the cost of continued stamina consumption, Jean can command the whirlwind to pull surrounding opponents and objects towards her front. Direction can be adjusted. Character is immobile during skill duration.

Gameplay Notes

  • When this skill hits at least one enemy, it generates 2.67 Elemental Particles.
  • This skill does not lift enemies more than 20 levels higher than Jean.

Constellation Effects

  • Constellation Level 1, Spiraling Tempest: "Increases the pulling speed of Gale Blade after holding for more than 1s, and increases the DMG dealt by 40%."
  • Constellation Level 5, Outbursting Gust: Increases this talent's level by 3 and raises its maximum level to 15.

Dandelion Breeze Elemental Burst

Calling upon the wind's protection, Jean creates a swirling Dandelion Field, launching surrounding opponents and dealing Anemo DMG.

At the same time, she instantly regenerates a large amount of HP for all party members. The amount of HP restored scales off Jean's ATK.

Dandelion Field

  • Continuously regenerates HP of characters within the AoE and continuously imbues them with Anemo.
  • Deals Anemo DMG to opponents entering or exiting the Dandelion Field.

Gameplay Notes

  • Dandelion Breeze's cast animation takes ~0.9 seconds to complete. Hence, Dandelion Field lasts for 9 seconds only.
  • All enemies within the field's AoE will take one instance of Entering/Exiting DMG when the field is created and when it dissipates.
  • All DMG dealt by this skill have no Internal Cooldown.
  • Additionally, due to the self-applied Anemo status, multiple Swirls can occur on the active character occur in between the casting and termination of the skill (around one every second) when affected by another element. This occurs without damage to the character. These multiple swirls have several uses:
    • It can also be used to cleanse certain debuffs from Ley Line Disorders as well as the Elemental Marks from Azhdaha. This can take several Swirls.
    • The Swirled element can be used to break elemental shields.
    • Damage to foes from these Swirls scales like any other Swirl damage â€” with Jean's level and elemental mastery
    • These cleansing swirls don't seem to activate 'elemental reaction' type buffs, like that from the four piece set of the Instructor set. (The Swirls on foes do).
  • Jean's Passive Talent 2, Let the Wind Lead, affects Dandelion Breeze as follows: "Using Dandelion Breeze will regenerate 20% of its Energy."

Wind Companion 1st Ascension Passive

Hits by Jean's Normal Attacks have a 50% chance to regenerate HP equal to 15% of Jean's ATK for all party members.

Gameplay Notes

  • Hits do not need to deal damage in order to heal.
  • Each individual hit has a 50% chance to heal, so successfully hitting multiple enemies can generate multiple instances of healing.
Let the Wind Lead 4th Ascension Passive

Using Dandelion Breeze will regenerate 20% of its Energy.

Gameplay Notes

  • This regenerates 16 energy.
Guiding Breeze Utility Passive

When a Perfect Cooking is achieved on a dish with restorative effects, Jean has a 12% chance to obtain double the product.

Gameplay Notes

  • If this talent activates while Jean cooks Mushroom Pizza, it is possible to double the amount of her special dish, Invigorating Pizza.
